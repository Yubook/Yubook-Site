    What is Yubook?

    We are building a Web3 peer-to-peer marketplace for for a variety of sectors. Starting with the beauty sector, we have already launched Fade and Glow apps (Beta), which enables professionals to transact directly with consumers.

    Through blockchain technology we will revolutionize IRL work-to-earn by incorporating Game-fi culture. Users can mint our NFTS and unlock token rewards for each successful booking. The more transactions professionals make, the more levels they unlock, which equals more rewards.

    Revenue Stream

    Users

    Yubook’s revenue model is relatively simple, as booking fees are the primary source of revenue. NFT minting is our next source of income, but professionals make most of the earnings in the app.

    Professionals

    There will be no fees for professionals to encourage massive enrolment and a high retention rate. A Gami-Fied work-and-earn initiative will encourage professionals to use Yubook.

    Unique NFT Utility

    Yubook will allow users to mint collectible NFT cards divided into 3 levels. They contain optional embedded rewards and perks for users who hold the NFT in their in-app wallet. Offers include:

    • Users - YUB tokens reward after each booking is complete.
    • Professionals - free NFT mint for professionals after level unlock.

    NFT Levels

    USER:
    Level 1 - Unlocks with Silver NFT = YUB rewards/booking.
    Level 2 - Unlocks with Gold NFT = x2 YUB rewards/booking.
    Level 3 - Unlocks with Diamond NFT = x3 YUB rewards/booking.

    PROFESSIONAL:
    Level 1 - Unlocks after 50 successful bookings = Silver NFT.
    Level 2 - Unlocks after 100 successful bookings = Gold NFT.
    Level 3 - Unlocks after 200 successful bookings = Diamond NFT.

    Staking Rewards

    Users who choose to hold YUB tokens can benefit from many staking opportunities within the platform. Yubook will offer direct “in-wallet” staking, “where rewards accumulate from maintaining” a positive YUB balance. This process allows them to receive daily dividends from their staked amount based on the APY. Practically, these staking programs will help to govern certain aspects of the network.
